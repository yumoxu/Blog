Intro to PMR

PMR: in contrast with MLPR

MLPR: predict things isolatedly.

Consider the coorelation between things, and the dependence of uncertainty.

PMR: modelling, learning and inference

Modelling: uncover the relations between: questions, latent factors, and measureable factors (observed data).

Learning: prior beliefs => model \& posterior distribution.

Inference: conditioned on the given variables to inference unknown variables.

Another perspective: we can regard learning as conditioned on the given data to learn parameters, which is also a higher-level "inference" process.
